Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an Oracle license?

No. All labs run on Oracle Cloud Free Tier which gives you $300 USD credit. Setup is covered in Session 1 on May 1st.

What if I miss a session?

Session recordings are available within the same week. You can catch up and still follow the next session without falling behind.

Can I connect to real Oracle HCM or ERP?

Free Tier provides OIC platform access. HCM and ERP adapter labs use REST mocks simulating Fusion payloads — the approach used in professional enterprise training globally.

Will I be ready for the certification?

That is the goal of this program. Week 4 is entirely dedicated to 1Z0-1042-25 prep — mock exams, practice questions, exam strategy, and timing. The exam itself (~$245 USD) is booked by you separately.

Is this suitable for freshers?

Yes, with basic IT knowledge — understanding of REST APIs, XML, and JSON is enough. No prior Oracle experience required. Week 1 covers all foundations from scratch.

I am outside India — can I join?

Absolutely. Students from UAE, UK, USA, Singapore and Australia are welcome. Payment via PayPal or wire transfer. 8–10 PM IST works well for Gulf and partially for UK.

What is the timing and how long is each class?

Classes run Mon–Fri, 8:00 PM to 10:00 PM IST — exactly 2 hours daily. 30 days total, 60 hours of live instruction. Platform is Graphy.

Only 10 seats — why so limited?

By design. Small batch means direct instructor access. Your questions get answered in the session, not ignored. Quality over volume for Batch 1.
